About Our Cause

Every year, countless broken devices and trash are discarded improperly. When electronic devices are thrown away, it contributes to e-waste. Pollution comes in three main forms:

  • Land Pollution - Waste discarded on land, such as trash and debris.
  • Water Pollution - Contaminants like plastics that pollute our oceans and rivers.
  • Air Pollution - Harmful gases and toxins released into the air, including those from e-waste.

When e-waste is improperly disposed of, rusted and broken electronics release toxic substances into the environment, affecting air quality.

How Can We Stop This?

We can reduce e-waste by recycling, selling old electronics instead of discarding them, and using platforms like Jawa to repurpose gadgets. This helps keep our environment clean and protects us from radiation and toxins.
